FAQ

Common questions answered

What is the 20% daily profit share?
At midnight JST each day, we calculate your net profit. If you're in profit, 20% is deducted from your charge balance. Loss days carry forward — they offset future profits before any share is calculated.
What happens if my balance runs out?
You'll receive a warning and have 24 hours to top up. If your balance is not replenished, the bot pauses until you recharge. No hidden fees or penalties.
How is the license fee charged?
The one-time license fee is automatically deducted from your first charge. For example, if you choose the $1,000 Starter plan and charge $3,000, your starting balance will be $2,000.
Can I run the bot on a cloud desktop (VPS)?
Yes. LAPLACE runs on any Windows 10/11 machine, including cloud desktops like AWS WorkSpaces, Paperspace, or Shadow PC.
What payment methods are accepted?
USDT (Tether) via TRC-20 (TRON) or ERC-20 (Ethereum). Payments are confirmed manually by our team, typically within 30 minutes.
Is my strategy safe?
The prediction engine runs entirely on our cloud servers. Your local client only handles bet execution. The algorithm is never exposed or distributed.
